The second chapter exists so you can feel the engine move.

Use the edge zones or a horizontal swipe to change chapters. Open the toolbar to resize type, cycle themes, or narrow the column. Long footnotes become a panel; on small screens they become a sheet.

__What this is not__

It is not a novel about archives. It is a short sample that shows how serial text, glossary marks[[1]], and document exhibits share one pipeline: parse → render → slots.

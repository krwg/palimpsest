You open a borrowed folio and the margin is crowded. Someone underlined a sentence, then another hand argued with the underline. The page keeps both.

This demo is **PalST** — short for Palimpsest — a reader engine for text that has layers worth keeping visible. Mark a word[[1]] and a footnote rises beside it, not buried at the back of a PDF.

***

__Shelf light__

— Did you come for the binding or the argument?

— Both. The argument left fingerprints.
